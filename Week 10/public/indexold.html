About Us

We believe that exceptional books do more than just tell good stories - they leave you mentally richer than when you started them. Book Rating System seeks out these gems from both established writers and first time authors - novels that whisk you to unfamiliar times and places, thrillers that are more than just 'page-turners', and thought-provoking non-fiction that entertains as it informs.
With thousands of books published each year, it can be mind-boggling to select a book. Book Rating System's mission is to provide readers with the ability to target in on the right book for them by age, genre. By providing a standardized rating system, readers can more easily navigate and evaluate their reading options. Movies have ratings…Television Shows have ratings…Games have ratings…and now, books have ratings, too.
